## How to Upload Videos (Re-edit Status)

### Step 1: Upload to Google Drive
1. Click the "Upload Video to Drive" button in the form
2. Or directly visit: https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1mIFaECu0YQ80tGDRiguYA7A4J4Hlefgy
3. Click "New" or drag-and-drop your video file
4. Upload your video (max 20MB for shorts)

### Step 2: Add Entry
1. Return to the dashboard
2. Fill out the form with vertical, exam, subject
3. Paste the YouTube link of the original video
4. Select "Re-edit" status
5. Click "Add Entry"

## File Size Limit

**Maximum:** 20MB per video file

This is the recommended size for YouTube Shorts.
